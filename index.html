Substrate Out of Pocket


SOOP is the chronic error at tool NGACS - MHS main tool. It is a frequent and common error. Furthermore, impact does not only at MHS module (MA, AU, APRT,... ) , SOOP can be connected to the quality issue of our down stream: BAM & CAM Module.

It contains many factor, such as Mechanical, Sofware, and Material, and a core factor of this error come from mechanical such as Bond Head, Voice Coil, SOOP Laser, and Conveyor. This core factor divide SOOP into 3 type of error (the other factors will not discuss in this chapter)

Real

Actually, substrate out of pocket & detected by SOOP Laser measurement.

  • Pick and Place System
    
- Failure Accuracy Criteria often: Perform required Calibration.
- Loosening Down Looking Camera sometimes: Replace Bond Head.
- Instability of Down Looking Camera rarely: Replace Bond Head.
- Loosening Bond Head's attached screw occasionally: Tighten by cap-head-hex 3mm/2Nm Torque.

  • The Conveyor System
    
- Lifter Plate not fully open/close e-spring often: Adjust air pressure valve on Lifter Plate.
 (SOOP at carrier's corner pockets)
- Lifter Plate up/down impact to planarity of carrier often: Replace Lifter Plate.
 (Lead to SOOP when open/close e-spring)

Fake

SOOP Laser measurement incorrect lead to wrong trigger.

  • SOOP Laser sensor
    
- Deviation of Laser Beam often: Perform Laser Calibration.
- Loosening of Laser sensor's Bracket sometimes: Replace new Bracket.
- Laser accuracy occasionally: Replace new Laser sensor.

  • Carrier's Process Position
    
- Loosening Conveyor Belt often: Tension belt or Replace belt.
- Incorrect Conveyor Relation Point often: Teaching paramters.
 (This parameter should be taught 1 time when setup machine)
- Distance between sensor and process position often: Adjust paramter.
 (Mostly, this is the consequences of other issue)
- Trigger threshold of sensor not reach fiducial sometimes: Teaching amplifier of conveyor's sensor.
 (Only for vertical sensor)
  • The Conveyor System
    
- Offset of Process area planarity over 0.2mm often: L3 Documentation.
- Offset of Process support planarity over 0.2mm often: L3 Documentation. 
- Planarity of conveyor system in respect to X/Y Axis system out of tolerance (304.5mm +/-0.3mm) nobodyverifylol: L3 Documentation.
- Parallelism of Conveyor-Axis system (Maximum offset of straightedge over 0.05mm) nobodyverifylol: L3 Documentation.

Escape

Basically, It's combination of 2 error Fake & Real SOOP.

Data

Mostly it's come from Deviation of Laser Beam.

Feeder Yield Issues


Yield in MHS has improved in Last 2 Years (Quality Commit). Major of rejected units come from The Feeder - The Complex Mechanism Part of ACS

Ironically, we don't have Vision Software to offset the deviation inside Feeder System. To deal with that, all replacing, adjusting or teaching event must be elaborate performed.

Module SBT51

Almost Normal Tracer/YI are the result of Tray was not located in SBT51 when placement units.

Factor Table
# Object Symptom Action
1 Material (Tray) damaged, bent... Reject or Hold to further investigation
2 Belt, O-ring damaged, worn out... Replace
3 Support roller higher than belt's surface Adjust position of roller - leveling with belt's surface
4 Conveyor (SBT34/SBT51) unbalance -Module SBT34: leveling Feeder
-Module SBT51: leveling Open/Close conveyor left & right side
Deviation between 4 point at 4 conveyor's corner less than 0.2mm
5 Lift-bench (in upper position) higher than processing tray Adjust gap between Lift-bench plane & SBT51 plane to "~1mm"
6 Alignment module SBT34 misalign to SBT51 Perform Up/Down align & Left/Right Alignment
7 Proximity Sensor (SBT51) Noise - not switching on/off right away Teach or Replace
Verify in ScopeView (sample size should be more than 10 trays)
8 Motor Card speed CS1/CS2: Adjustable - CS3: 8 - CS4: 0
CS1: Slow Speed (Signal #5 trigger) CS2: Fast Speed (Signal #5 & #7 trigger)
9 Sliding guide rough surface Remove to grinding.

Validation

- Loading single tray to feeder at least 10 times to measurement the transport time from SBT34's downstream sensor to SBT51. tolerance between each time should be "1.7s +/-0.1s"
Use ScopeView
- Tray isn't bounce back after hitting stopper. (Monitor by DLC)

Lifter

  • Lift-bench
    
- Units OOP when tray was fell down from SBT51 to Lift-bench's upper position often: Adjust gap to "1mm".
- Gripper Cylinder malfunction sometimes: Replace.
- Shock Absorber of Lift-bench malfunction sometimes: Replace.

  • Lift 2 (SBT35)
    
- Shock Absorber of Lift 2 malfunction often: Replace
- Movement speed is too fast sometimes: Adjust air pressure valve
- Gap between Lift & Feeder's wall rarely: Adjust gap
  • The Conveyor System
    
- Over-clamp at SBT51 sometimes: Adjust Open-end & Closed-end of clamper.
- Open/Close conveyor is interrupted occasionally: Tighten screw & add washer/ lock washer.
